IDENTIFICATION OF UTILIZATION OF DRINKING WATER RESOURCES IN AN AREA
(NANDALAGUNTA,KOVUR,NELLORE)


Abstract

The main objective of this project is to identify the usage of drinking water in rural area called Nandalagunta,SPSR Nellore. We as a group of three members interacted with the people iving in nandalagunta and enquired them about the usage of drinking water resouces. Through this project we are able to identify which drinking water resource is mostly being used in that area. In regarding that, we impemented a working model which is a website to create an awarness on diseases caused by wrong consumption of drinking water resources.

Introduction

Drinking water is water that is used in drink or food preparation,potable water is water that is safe to be used as drinking water. The amount of drinking water required to maintain good health varies, and depends on physical activity level, age, health-related issues, and environmental conditions. Recent work showed that the most important driver of water turnover which is closely linked to water requirements is energy expenditure. For those who work in a hot climate, up to 16 litres (4.2 US gal) a day may be required.

Conclusion

It has been identified from the survey that 74% of people using mineral water and 26% of people using general water.
• Majority of people are using mineral water and minoriy of people are using general water.
• Some of the them claimed that they are using municipal water as it is the free drinking water resource.
• Most of the natives claimed that they are using mineral water because ground water may contain contaminants and can cause several harmful diseases.
In regarding that, we implemented a working model which is a website to create an awareness on diseases caused by wrong consumption of drinking water in our absence.
